What SingLift is for

SingLift is designed for long-tone practice, vocal training, pitch stability observation, and daily repetition. It is not a pitch scoring app and it does not attempt to judge musical performance.

Instead, SingLift helps users observe vocal steadiness, sustained voice behavior, and repeatable control in a simple, instrument-style format.

SingLift is intended as a practice and observation tool. It is not a medical, diagnostic, or professional evaluation device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the meter move during vibrato?

This is expected behavior. Vibrato includes intentional variation, which may appear as natural movement in the response.

Why does the value drop?

Short interruptions, unstable pitch, background noise, or microphone positioning may affect the response.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Voice analysis is processed locally on your device during use.

Is this a pitch scoring app?

No. SingLift is designed to observe vocal steadiness and repeatability, not musical scoring.
