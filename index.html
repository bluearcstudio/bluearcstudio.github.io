About Blue Arc Studio

Blue Arc Studio is an independent software studio developing focused digital tools for practical use.

Our first public product is SingLift, a vocal stability instrument for observing voice steadiness in real time during focused vocal practice.

Products

SingLift is a vocal stability instrument for Android. It helps users observe vocal stability and voice steadiness in real time through a calm, instrument-style interface.

SingLift is designed for long-tone practice, vocal training, pitch stability observation, and focused daily practice.
